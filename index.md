## About Me

I am currently a researcher focusing on <strong><span style="color:#39c;">autonomous driving systems</span></strong>, robotics, and intelligent sensing technologies. I hold a PhD in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from <strong><span style="color:#39c;">The University of Hong Kong</span></strong>, where my research focused on contactless electromagnetic sensing, power electronics, and intelligent fault diagnosis for energy systems.

My current work centers on <strong><span style="color:#39c;">end-to-end autonomous driving development</span></strong>, spanning perception, localization, motion planning, control, and system integration. I have led and contributed to multiple <strong><span style="color:#39c;">real-world autonomous vehicle and robotics projects</span></strong>, including a methanol fuel cell autonomous delivery vehicle, autonomous parking and docking systems, follow-me electric vehicles, and vision-based transport robots. I have extensive experience bridging <strong><span style="color:#39c;">simulation and real-world deployment</span></strong>, using CARLA, ROS, and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) platforms, and deploying autonomous systems on embedded NVIDIA Orin domain controllers.

I am passionate about transforming <strong><span style="color:#39c;">AI-driven sensing and autonomous driving research</span></strong> into <strong><span style="color:#39c;">robust, deployable systems</span></strong> for real-world applications.
